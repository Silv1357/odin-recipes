Banana Pudding

Description

This dish is a cold dessert, using primarily bananas. It is often mildly sweet and always chilled, a yummy treat for the summer!

Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • condensed milk
  • vanilla pudding mix
  • skim milk
  • vanilla extract
  • whipped cream
  • vanilla wafers
  • bananas
    •

    Steps

  • Gather all ingredients.
  • Beat cream cheese in bowl until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, then pudding mix. Gradually mix in cold milk until smooth, followed by vanilla.
  • Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
  • Line bottom of a rectangular dish with vanilla wafers.
  • Arrange sliced bananas evenly on top.
  • Cover with pudding mixture.
  • Top with remaining whipped topping.
  • Cover and chill in the fridge for at least 3 hours before serving.
